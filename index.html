Hello and Welcome to Geo Path Finder

These are the directions:

  1. Step 1: When you click the button, the 'start' country will light up red and the 'end' country will light up blue.
  2. Step 2: The objective of the game is to try and connect these 2 countries by guessing all the countries in between.
  3. Step 3: The game ends when you connect the 2 countries.
  4. Step 4: Try to get it in as little guesses as possible.
  5. Step 5: Start the game when you are ready!!
